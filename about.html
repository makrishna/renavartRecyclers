About

Globally, technology has become increasingly reliant, and the need for sustainable electronic waste disposal has never been more pressing. As Mahatma Gandhi once said, “The earth provides enough to satisfy every man’s needs, but not every man’s greed”. With this quote in our mind, we strive to make a positive impact on our environment and local communities through our work in E-Waste Management and Recycling.

Renavart Recyclers India Private Limited has incorporated on 21 st November 2022 in Telangana, India that focuses on e-waste management and recycling with a mission to create a sustainable future that emphasizes its commitment to environmental sustainability. Our team of experienced professionals is dedicated to ensuring that every item of electronic waste is disposed of in a responsible and sustainable manner, while also providing a range of value-added services that help our clients optimize their resources and reduce their carbon footprint. We believe that every piece of E-Waste has the potential to be transformed into a valuable resource, and we are committed to providing safe and reliable solutions for our clients.

LOGO Description

The word “Renavart” (रे नावर्ट ) means ‘Molecules’ (Renu) in ‘Circular Motion’ (Avartan) and is derived from Sanskrit, an ancient Indian language. The logo of “Renavart” is a symbolic representation of ‘E-Waste’ (Bottom Squares) recycled into ‘raw material’ (Upper Squares), and the entire process is represented as a “Circular Economy”. Through the latest technology and our data-driven strategy, we want to change electronic waste management in India.

Sustainability

Renavart Recyclers are deeply committed to environmental sustainability and responsible e-waste management & recycling practices. We believe that by properly managing electronic waste, we can help to protect the environment, conserve natural resources, and reduce the negative impact of electronic waste on human health and the planet. To achieve these goals, we have implemented a number of sustainable practices throughout our operations. We work to minimize our carbon footprint by utilizing energy-efficient technologies and reducing waste wherever possible.

In addition, we adhere to the highest standards of safety and compliance to ensure that all electronic waste is handled and disposed of in a responsible and environmentally-friendly manner. We employ best practices for electronic waste collection, disposal, and recycling.

Our commitment to sustainability goes beyond our operations, as we also work closely with our clients to promote responsible e-waste management practices. We offer education and outreach programs to help our clients understand the importance of electronic waste management and provide guidance on how to properly dispose of electronic waste.

At our company, we believe that responsible e-waste management is a critical component of environmental sustainability. We are proud to be at the forefront of this important industry and remain dedicated to promoting sustainability and protecting the environment for future generations.
